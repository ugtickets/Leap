Account Types

AfricanBooks.com features three account types; Reader Accounts, Author Accounts and Publisher Accounts

Reader Accounts are BUYING Accounts; these are people who buy and read books through our e-commerce platform: AfricanBooks.com

Authors & Publisher accounts are SELLING accounts. These accounts give users the ability to sell and earn royalties on their own uploaded book content through our publishing platform: AfricanBooks.pub.

If you are the author of a book or a series of books, you would need to open an AUTHOR’s Account.

If you are a registered publishing company legally representing several different authors, you would need to open a PUBLISHER Account. Please note that publishers will be required to upload a scanned copy of their author/publisher agreement, showing proof that they are legally allowed to publish books on behalf of their authors.

Creating an Account

It is FREE to create an Author Account on AfricanBooks.com. Yes, we said FREE! All you need to do is fill in the required information on the signup page to get started today.

Managing your account

Understanding Your Dashboard: The Author’s dashboard is the control panel for your account. This is where you can create and manage your profile, track your activity on our site, change your settings, upload and manage your books, track your royalty payments and so much more.

Managing Your Author’s Profile: As an author/publisher you have both a public and private profile. Your private profile refers to those details that we collect from you regarding your identity, your payment information, contact information, etc. Your public profile on the other hand, shows your readers who you are as an author, and includes information about how many books you have available, your writing style and what makes you unique as an author. For more information on how to create your public profile, visit the ‘Create My Public Profile’ information guide.

Managing Your Comments, Ratings and Reviews: It is important to know what your readers have to say. In the comments and ratings sections, we provide valuable feedback from your buyers on what they think about your book and comments they have for you as the author. This is a communication portal for you, giving you direct access to those who matter most, your customers!

Managing Your Settings: In the settings section, you have full control over the functionality of your dashboard. You can change your password, update your email, change your privacy and notification settings and much more. Ensure your account is up-to-date so that you don’t miss a beat.

The Author's Bookshelf

In your dashboard, you will find a drop-down menu called ‘Bookshelf.’ This is where all of your book content lives within our store. Here you can add books to sell, set and change your book pricing, offer book promotional offers, view your published and unpublished books, track your book sales and read reviews. You can find more detailed information on uploading book content under the ‘Publish Your Book’ learning tab.

Creating a Public Profile

This is an important aspect of your account because it shares with your readers, and potential readers, who you are. Give a bit of information about yourself including your writing style and what makes you unique as an author. Include a picture so your readers can see who you are as well. Your public profile will automatically link readers to the books you have published on AfricanBooks.com for easy access. It will also show your readers your ratings from other readers just like them. It is important that you keep your profile updated so that your readers can follow with you as you grow from a new author to a best-selling global sensation!
