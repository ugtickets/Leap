Community GuidelinesAfricanBooks.com

AfricanBooks.com wants your voice to be heard! As such, our websites (AfricanBooks.com/AfricanBooks.pub) provide various features for engaging and encouraging readers and users to share authentic feedback about the books, authors and publishers providing content through the AfricanBooks.com platforms – both positive or negative. These Community Guidelines will help you use Community features, including Customer Reviews, Book Ratings, Customer Questions & Answers, Authors Public Profile pages among others.

NOTE: These Guidelines do not apply to the content included within the Books sold on AfricanBooks.com, which are subject to separate policies and content guidelines.

We take the integrity of the Community seriously. Any attempt to manipulate Community content or features, including; by contributing false, misleading, or inauthentic ratings, reviews or comments is strictly prohibited. If you violate our Guidelines, we may restrict your ability to use Community features, remove content, unpublish related Books, suspend or terminate your account. If we determine that an AfricanBooks.com account has been used to engage in any form of misconduct, remittances and payments may be withheld or permanently forfeited. Misconduct may also violate local Ugandan or international laws which can lead to legal action, civil and/or criminal penalties.

We encourage anyone who suspects that content manipulation is taking place or that our Guidelines are being violated in any way to notify us at: admin@africanbooks.com. We investigate concerns thoroughly and take any appropriate actions as reasonable determined by our legal team.

By accessing or using our Community features, you agree to our Conditions of Use and to abide by these Guidelines, as modified from time to time. These Guidelines apply to any content (including text, images, audio, video, and links) you submit to AfricanBooks.com and actions you take (such as the rating of books, providing of feedback, etc.) when using Community features. These Guidelines also apply to your interactions with other members of the Community.

Eligibilty

To contribute to Customer features (for example, Customer Reviews, Customer Questions, Customer Answers, and Book Ratings) you must be a registered user of AfricanBooks.com and have verifiable proof of purchase (ie: ABC order history) of the content in which you are reviewing.

Be Helpful and Relevant

The Community is intended to provide helpful, relevant content to customers and/or potential customers. Content you submit should be relevant and based on your own honest opinions and experience.

Customer Reviews, Book Ratings, Questions and Answers should be about the specific Book you are reviewing only and can include feedback on the book’s writing style, readability, formatting, storyline, etc.

Feedback about the author and/or publisher of the Book should be shared on the Author’s Public Profile Page if the content you are sharing is solely about the author and not about a particular work. This can include information about an author’s ideology, general writing style or overall book offerings.

Respect Others

AfricanBooks.com values diverse opinions, so you may disagree with some content that you come across. Keep in mind that something that may be disagreeable to you may not violate our Guidelines. In order to maintain a welcoming environment for all users, your participation in the Community must always be respectful of others. In particular:

Don't post content that is defamatory, harassing, threatening, or inflammatory. For example, don't use obscenities or profanity, and don't express hatred or intolerance for people on the basis of race, ethnicity, nationality, gender, religion, age, or ability.
Don't post content that is obscene, pornographic, or lewd, or that contains nudity or sexually explicit images.
Don't post content that invades others' privacy. For example, don't post other people's phone numbers, email addresses, mailing addresses, or other personal information, and don't post links to phishing or other malware sites.
Don't impersonate other people or organizations or pretend to be someone or something you're not.
Don't repeatedly send messages or requests to other people.
Don't attempt to drown out other people's opinions, including by posting from multiple accounts or coordinating with others.
Don't engage in name-calling or attack people based on whether you agree with them.
You may question the beliefs and expertise of others as long as it is relevant and done in a respectful and non-threatening manner.

Promotions and Commercial Solicitations

In order to preserve the integrity of Community content, Book content and author profiles. Any posted content consisting of advertising, promotion, or solicitation (whether direct or indirect) is not allowed, including:

Offering compensation or requesting compensation (including free or discounted products) in exchange for positive ratings, reviews or comments on your Books.
Posting advertisements or links for people to buy the same Books on other websites, online stores, or through private means.

Please note that Book authors and publishers may continue to provide free or discounted copies of their books to readers, as long as the author or publisher does not require a review in exchange or attempt to influence the review or rating of their book.

Sexual Content

Products containing explicit sexual content and/or sensuality are not permitted on AfricanBooks.com. We encourage readers to notify us if any book on our site violates this guideline so that we can review it and act accordingly.

Infringing Content

Don't post content or interact with other members of the Community in a way that infringes the intellectual property or other proprietary rights of others. Only post your own content or content that you have legal permission to use.

Illegal Activity

Don't post content that encourages or supports behavior that is illegal, including violence, fraud, illegal drug use, underage drinking, or child abuse. Examples of prohibited content include:

Content advocating, threatening, or joking about physical or financial harm to yourself or others, including terrorism.
Content promoting fraudulent goods, services, schemes, or promotions (e.g., make-money-fast schemes, pyramid schemes).
Content posted intentionally as sarcasm as it relates to self-harm or harm to others.

Additional Guidelines for Customer Reviews

The following guidelines apply to Customer Reviews in addition to the other guidelines given above:

If your review or rating is removed or rejected because it does not comply with our guidelines concerning promotional content, you may not resubmit a review on the same Book, even if the resubmitted review includes different content.
We may restrict the ability to submit a review or rating when we detect unusual reviewing behavior, or to maintain the best possible shopping experience.

Reporting Abuse

For some types of content, including customer Ratings, reviews and answers, you can report possible violations of these Guidelines by screenshotting the potential abuse and emailing it, along with your concerns to admin@africanbooks.com. Please specifying the location of the content and the reason you believe it violates these Guidelines in your email.

If you receive an offer for compensation of any kind (including free or discounted products, refunds, or reimbursements) in exchange for creating, modifying, or posting content in violation of these Guidelines, forward the offer, including contact information and the AfricanBooks.com Book listing admin@africanbooks.com
